Subject Omega

Subject Name: Drako Vishious

D.O.B/C: April 26th

Gender: Male

Species: Patherera uncia (Snow Leopard), and Homo sanguinis venator (Vampire Warrior) Hybrid.


Personality:

Likes: Rock Music, RPGs, FPS games, City Builders, Stuffed Crust Pizza, Eating Meat, Sleep, Being referred to as a vampire Cat or even the king of vampire cats.
Dislikes: Most Country Music, Green Vegetables, Gotcha Games, Waking up early, being referred to as an abomination.



Physical Description:

Height: 5'
Weight: 200lbs/90.7kg
Hair/Fur Color: Greyish Black
Skin Color: Pale with light blue highlights
Eye Color: Golden Main Iris, Red around the pupil.
Pupil Type: Slit



Physical

Stamina: Subject's Stamina is that of a warrior cast vampire's stamina.
Strength: Subject's Strength is twice that of a human and equivalent of that of a warrior cast vampire.
Speed: Subject can run approximately as fast as a human can in long stretches, however in short bursts he is half again as fast.
Perception: Subject has great night vision but does have a sunlight sensitivity to his vision.
Hightened Senses: Subject has hightened senses of sound and smell.


Abilities:

Sunlight: Subject is impervious to sunlight similar to humans, the vampire characteristic of the sun allergy did not get passed to the subject.
Intellect: Subject is highly intellegent but lacks social skills a normal vampire or human would have, this is most likely due to his laboratory upbringing and could be considered autistic by human standards.
Telepathy: Subject's Telepathic range is approximately 30'
Telekinesis: Subject has no telekinetic ability. This trait failed to pass on to the subject.
Dematerilaization: Subject did not inherit the vampire ability to teleport.
Shapeshifting: Subject has the ability to shapeshift similar to that of a lycan. He has three forms he can change into; An animal form, a humaniod form with animal ears and tail (Refered to him as his neko form), and a therianthropic hybrid form (Could be mistaken for wereleopard).
Mist Calling: Subject can call forth a thick mist that can travers the ground up to and over five feet of mist, tests are ongoing about this mysterious ability.
